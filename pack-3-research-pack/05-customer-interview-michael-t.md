MAYA: Michael, thanks for the time. I want to understand why you've slowed down ordering — we pulled you in because our system flagged you as a lapsing repeat customer.

MICHAEL: It's made me want to go back to the store and actually feel things before I buy them. For sheets I'll keep ordering from you — I know my size, I know the color, I trust the photography. MAYA: If we had a retail store in Denver, would that change things?

MICHAEL: One hundred percent. The closest thing is that Parachute-adjacent popup in Cherry Creek, and I've been there. Yeah, if you had a real Haven & Hearth store in Denver I'd go.
